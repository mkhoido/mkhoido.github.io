ABOUT ME

Growing up in Tampa, Florida, I always had an eye for visual communication. Even in elementary school I was collecting video game art, scouring and admiring video game package design and instruction booklets, comparing fast food bag designs and junk food packaging. Growing up in Tampa also meant I was surrounded by sports, so it was natural that I grew an affinity for comparing team branding even as a child. I was obssessed with anything visually appealing, and fell in love with design.

I started honing basic graphic and coding skills as early as middle school as a means to kill time. From making mock web layouts and logos for fictious companies, to making amateur visual art, it was a hobby I truly enjoyed. I felt safe here and found passion in a skill that I wanted to devote time to crafting.

Not wanting to limit myself to purely graphic design, I branched out of Photoshop to master the entire suite, from InDesign to Illustrator.

I attended the University of Florida in Gainesville to pursue a bachelors in Advertising. With my repitore in Art Direction, I got to hone the craft of branding, to social media management. In a fast paced, ever changing world we livei in, it felt imperative to develop these highly relevant skills.

I am currently pursuing a graduate certificate in Web Design, as a way to truly round out my skill set. I don't want to just design pure graphics but to learn how to build web content through coding languages such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
